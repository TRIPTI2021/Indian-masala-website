ABOUT US:

The birth of M.Motilal Masalawala - Grant Road in 1912 was revolutionary in the Masala
industry. It created history at a time when women used to grind masalas make pickles and
papads in their homes. Our forefather Shri.Mangaldas won their hearts and trust by introducing ready
mix masalas, wide range of spices of India, papads and pickles. Started as a small setup in Mumbai
Today our products are available worldwide. We are always striving to make quality products with
'Mangal Tradition'.
